About me

"A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one." - William Shakespeare. My education spans across an array of industries. This give you insight into my belief of being a dynamic and adaptive individual. Throughout my career - as an accounts clerk, Interlink Project Assistant, Bulk Administrator Contractor and currently a Financial Operations Administrator - I often felt that my career could be more lively and interesting.

I often heard the term "coding", however, I was unsure of what it was and how one could get into it. A family member referred me to HyperionDev, an institution that could give insight and teach me how to code, through their Software Engineering bootcamp. Since taking up the course, I must say I have found what I have been longing for - a dynamic and challenging industry with an endless scope of possibilities.

The bootcamp has exposed me to the Python and Java programming languages, interacting with databases through SQL, the basics of HTML and CSS as well as concepts surrounding machine learning. Upon completion of this bootcamp, I aim to set foot into the tech industry and expose myself to the limitless programming languages with the aim to further my professional and personal growth.
